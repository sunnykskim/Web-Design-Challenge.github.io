Wind Speed


Wind speed, uniformly reporting low (less than 15mph on average) tends to experience anomalies at a higher frequency in cities with higher latitude. This is likely for the same reason there is a bias of higher humidity in cities with higher latitude. The data is as of January, therefore the northern hemisphere is experiencing Winter, which means there are higher chances for storms.
